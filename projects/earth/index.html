«Earth»

(2021)

Vases (chamotte, colored clay)

Completed at ETPK Vorontsovo in 2021.

Shown on December 28, 2021 in the Vorontsovo ETPK on the report of the creative team.

Exhibited at the exhibition “Tryn-trava” (Devil-may-care) in October 2022 (Museum-estate of the Muravyov Apostles).

Was presented at the New Year's Fair "???" in December 2022.

Exhibited at the Christmas Exhibition of the Moscow Union of Artists until January 2023 (Kuznetsky Most 20).
